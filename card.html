Puppy Dog

A puppy is a juvenile dog. Some puppies can weigh 1-1.5 kg (1-3 lb), while larger ones can weigh up to 7-11 kg (15-23 lb).

  • Breed: Yorkshire Terrier
  • Gestation: Born after 63 Days
  • Companionship: Can be as a Pet
  • Skill: Exceptional rat catching abilities

Puppies are highly social animals and spend most of their waking hours interacting with either their mother or littermates.
